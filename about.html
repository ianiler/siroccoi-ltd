Our Story

Since its inception in 2005, Siroccoi (U) Ltd has been a leading provider of engineering and construction services across Uganda. From hydro-mechanical projects to industrial solutions, we pride ourselves on innovation, precision, and sustainability.

  • Over a decade of expertise in hydro-mechanical installations.
  • Trusted for industrial coatings, thermal insulation, and maintenance services.
  • Dedicated to environmentally sustainable engineering solutions.

Siroccoi is dedicated to creating world-class engineering solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of industry leaders and stakeholders.
